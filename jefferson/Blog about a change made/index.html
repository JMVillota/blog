Things you stopped doing

11/11/2021
3 minute read

C hanges are necessary because they help us to grow and mature, as well as to appreciate what is true. But we are so used to what we do that it becomes difficult for us to break with what affects us; we prefer not to fight and let others impose their conditions.

W hen I was a child I wanted to be older, now that I am older I want to be small, it is one of the changes in the way of thinking that I have had, since now I would like time to pass more slowly, because time easily escapes us. I don't have time to play with my friends, at first it was nice, but now I don't want time to pass so quickly.

A fter that, very good things have also happened to me growing up, I learned to stop saying yes or no, by default. It comes to a moment where I have much clearer when to say yes and when to say no, for my own good and for the good of others, because deep down saying yes or no to make others happy is not valid for them either if I It makes me unhappy, at the beginning I learned to stop punishing myself and blaming myself for the things that happen to me. Yes, I am imperfect, yes, I make the same mistakes over and over again, then I stopped judging everything and everyone, and began to empathize and understand, also myself.

S oon I will finish my career and I will be a software engineer that will be a big change in my life, but that will happen after a while, for now I want to lose my fear and insecurity. What's the worst that can happen to me? Apart from death, the suffering of the people I love and the deep losses, what else can happen that has not already happened? Because when you are one age you have already gone through many difficult personal circumstances, and you can better assimilate them shape, and that's what I'm trying to change about myself for now.

P hysically, I can say that in these last two years of the pandemic, I have gained a little weight, but it is not something that I worry about, I am happy as I am.

A nd finally I can say that I am aware that I am in one of the best times of my life, with very little backpack on my back, and with enough energy and enthusiasm still to live in a "adolescent" way most of the time.

I am..
